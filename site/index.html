Little Card

Little Card is a small Android app that turns a YouTube link into a nice-looking image that can be shared on Instagram Stories.

Have you ever wanted to show a cool YouTube video to your friends through Instagram Stories? You can share a link, true, but there's no visual preview. You could take screenshots and cobble something together, but that gets old real fast.

Little Card removes that hassle: share a YouTube or YouTube Music link with the app, and it generates a clean card image with the video's title, channel name, and thumbnail.

The app is not yet listed in the Google Play Store. Drop me a line if you'd like to try it (closed testing is underway).
