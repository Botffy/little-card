Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-12-13

This Privacy Policy explains how the Little Card app handles data.

1. Summary

Little Card does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data. All processing happens entirely on your device.

2. Data the App Handles

When you share a YouTube link with the app, Little Card:

  • extracts the video ID from the link,
  • fetches public video metadata from the official YouTube Data API (title, thumbnail, uploader),
  • generates an image on your device,
  • copies the YouTube URL to your clipboard for convenience,
  • allows you to share the generated image using Android's standard sharing system.

3. No External Servers

Little Card:

  • does not send your data to my servers (I operate none),
  • does not use analytics,
  • does not use advertisements,
  • does not store logs,
  • does not track your usage.

All processing stays on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

The app depends on:

  • the YouTube Data API to retrieve public video metadata,
  • Android's clipboard (local only),
  • Android's share sheet (your chosen app handles the image).

These services may have their own privacy policies.

5. Permissions

Little Card only uses:

  • Internet access (to fetch public video info),
  • Temporary local storage (cache),
  • Clipboard access (to copy the share URL).

The app does not request access to files, contacts, location, camera, microphone, or any sensitive permissions.

6. Children's Privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and does not collect data.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Updates take effect when published.
